Are You Prepared for Coding Bootcamp?

Welcome

If you've made it this far you have some interest in pursing a career in coding. This website is to help you understand how to recognize what level of coding you are at and to help you prepare for a coding bootcamp if you desire to join one. This website will give you information on whether you are a novice/beginner, intermediate, or expert coder. Below there is information about each level, with a quiz included. Please take your time, read and happy coding!
